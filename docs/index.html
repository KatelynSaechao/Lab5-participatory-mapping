Mapping Nature Experiences in Seattle

Created by: Katelyn Saechao

This map allows people to map out their experiences in nature in the Seattle area! To get started, click the location on the map and simply enter your name, the date and time you visited this nature location, and your description of your experience at this place in nature. To read the experiences on the map, hover your mouse over the tree location marker!

The template used to create this participatory map can be found here.
